Level System, Quests & Rewards:

This website features a Level System, Quest System, and Rewards System that enhance your browsing experience.

How it works:
All data (XP, Level, Gold, Quest Progress, Purchased Rewards) is stored exclusively in your browser's Local Storage.

Privacy & Data Storage:
• No data is sent to any server
• No data is stored on external servers
• All data remains on your device
• We don't collect, track, or analyze any of this data

Important:
Since all data is stored locally in your browser, you will lose all progress (XP, Level, Gold, Quest completions, purchased rewards) if you:
• Clear your browser cache completely
• Use private/incognito mode (data is cleared when you close the window)
• Clear browser data manually
• Use a different browser or device

Your progress is tied to your browser and device — it's completely local and private!
