Storage Backup & Reset

Manage your Local Storage data from Arijkx | Pixels

Download Backup

Creates a backup file containing all your saved data: your current Level, XP, Gold, completed Quests, and all unlocked Rewards. The backup is saved as a JSON file on your computer. You can use this backup later to restore your progress if needed.

Upload & Restore Backup

Restores your saved data from a previously downloaded backup file. This will overwrite all your current data with the data from the backup file. Your current Level, XP, Gold, Quest progress, and unlocked Rewards will be replaced with the values from the backup.

Reset All Data

WARNING: This action cannot be undone! This will permanently delete ALL your saved data:

  • Your Level and all accumulated XP
  • All your Gold
  • All Quest progress (all quests will be reset to uncompleted)
  • All unlocked Rewards (you will need to unlock them again with Gold)
  • First visit flag

After resetting, you will start completely fresh. Make sure to download a backup before resetting if you want to keep your progress!
